# Anger is the enemy of wisdom

![bird in the sky](/blog/img/bird-in-the-sky.jpg)

As a normal human-being, I am as same as others susceptible to emotional fluctuation. Angry temper arises from time to time, which is not what I desire. How can we get out of the emotional flutter?

This week, I was agitated due to a chaotic incidence at work. The emotion burst out of my mind instantly that there wasn't any time to react to my anger. At that moment, it was like a volcano exploded, splattering lava into sky, flowing downhill into forest and destroying all the living creatures on its way. Till the explosion calmed down, there was only lifeless silence burying all my regrets.
