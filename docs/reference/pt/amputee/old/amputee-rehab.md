### 常見截肢原因 **Common Causes**

| 原因 Cause                          | 說明 Description                                                                                       |
| :---------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **血管疾病 Vascular disease**       | 糖尿病足、周邊動脈疾病；最常見之截肢原因 Diabetic foot, peripheral arterial disease; most common cause |
| **外傷 Trauma**                     | 交通事故、工業意外、戰傷 Vehicle accidents, industrial injuries, combat injuries                       |

## 截肢層級分類

截肢層級直接影響後續義肢選配、步態模式與功能預後。治療師須熟悉各層級之生物力學特性。

### 下肢截肢 **Lower Limb Amputation**

| 層級 Level                        | 截肢位置 Location                | 生物力學特點 Biomechanical Features                                          | 步態預後 Gait Prognosis                                                  |
| :-------------------------------- | :------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **賽姆截肢 Syme's**               | 踝關節離斷 Ankle disarticulation | 保留足跟墊，可完全承重 Preserved heel pad, full weight-bearing               | 優良；步態對稱性高 Excellent; high gait symmetry                         |
| **經踝截肢 Transtibial (TT)**     | 膝下 Below-knee                  | 保留膝關節，能量消耗增加 25-40% Preserved knee, energy expenditure ↑ 25-40%  | 良好；約 80% 恢復社區步行 Good; ~80% return to community ambulation      |
| **經膝截肢 Knee disarticulation** | 膝關節離斷 Knee disarticulation  | 保留股骨長度，承重面積佳 Preserved femur length, good weight-bearing surface | 中等至良好 Moderate to good                                              |
| **經股截肢 Transfemoral (TF)**    | 膝上 Above-knee                  | 失去膝關節，能量消耗增加 60-100% Loss of knee, energy expenditure ↑ 60-100%  | 中等；約 50% 恢復社區步行 Moderate; ~50% return to community ambulation  |
| **半骨盆截肢 Hemi-pelvectomy**    | 半側骨盆移除 Hemipelvis removal  | 核心穩定度大幅降低 Significantly reduced core stability                      | 挑戰性高；輪椅為主要移動方式 Challenging; wheelchair as primary mobility |

### 上肢截肢 **Upper Limb Amputation**

| 層級 Level                              | 截肢位置 Location                           | 功能影響 Functional Impact                                     | 義肢需求 Prosthetic Needs                               |
| :-------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------ |
| **經腕截肢 Transradial (TR)**           | 前臂 Below-elbow                            | 保留肘關節，旋前旋後功能 Preserved elbow, pronation/supination | 美觀義肢、肌電義肢 Cosmetic, myoelectric prosthesis     |
| **經肘截肢 Elbow disarticulation**      | 肘關節離斷 Elbow disarticulation            | 保留上臂長度 Preserved humerus length                          | 客製化義肢 Custom prosthesis                            |
| **經肱截肢 Transhumeral (TH)**          | 上臂 Above-elbow                            | 失去肘關節與前臂 Loss of elbow and forearm                     | 雙動態義肢或體外動力義肢 BDD or body-powered prosthesis |
| **肩關節離斷 Shoulder disarticulation** | 肩關節離斷 Shoulder disarticulation         | 上肢功能幾乎全失 Near-total loss of upper limb function        | 肩背懸吊義肢 Shoulder-suspended prosthesis              |
| **半側肩胛胸廓離斷 Forequarter**        | 鎖骨與肩胛骨移除 Clavicle & scapula removal | 無力矩可用 No available lever arm                              | 輔具為主 Assistive devices prioritized                  |

::: warning ⚠️ 截肢層級與功能預後 Level-Function Relationship
截肢層級越高（近端），功能影響越大，義肢使用率越低。經股截肢者僅約 50% 能使用義肢進行社區步行，而經踝截肢者可達 80% 以上。
Higher (more proximal) amputation levels result in greater functional impact and lower prosthesis usage rates. Only about 50% of transfemoral amputees achieve community ambulation with a prosthesis, compared to over 80% for transtibial amputees.
:::

## 術前照護 **Pre-prosthetic Care**

### 殘肢照護 **Residual Limb Care**

術後殘肢的品質直接影響義肢穿戴的成功率。良好的殘肢照護應包括：

The quality of the residual limb directly impacts prosthetic fitting success. Optimal residual limb care includes:

- **皮膚完整性維持** Skin integrity maintenance：每日檢查殘肢皮膚，留意紅疹、潰瘍、繭 formation
- **殘肢塑型** Limb shaping：使用彈性繃帶（elastic bandage）或矽膠套筒（silicone liner）促進錐形塑型
- **關節活動度維持** ROM maintenance：每日執行所有保留關節之全關節活動度（PROM/AROM）運動
- **水腫控制** Edema control：加壓包紮、 elevation、早期活動

### 去敏感化訓練 **Desensitization Program**

殘肢的感覺過敏（hypersensitivity）是常見問題，可影響義肢穿戴舒適度。去敏感化訓練方案：

Residual limb hypersensitivity is common and can impair prosthetic comfort. Desensitization programs include:

| 訓練方法 Modality             | 操作方式 Technique                                                   | 頻率 Frequency                              |
| :---------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------ |
| **震動刺激 Vibration**        | 不同頻率震動器接觸殘肢 Distinct frequency vibrators to residual limb | 3-5 次/天，每次 5 分鐘 3-5x/day, 5 min each |
| **輕觸 Light touch**          | 棉花、毛刷、不同材質交替 Cotton, brush, varied textures alternating  | 2-3 次/天 2-3x/day                          |
| **加壓按摩 Pressure massage** | 由近端向遠端之漸進式壓力 Progressive proximal-to-distal pressure     | 2 次/天，每次 10 分鐘 2x/day, 10 min        |
| **敲擊 Tapping**              | 殘肢表面輕柔敲擊 Gentle tapping over residual limb surface           | 3 次/天 3x/day                              |
| **漸進式負重 Progressive WB** | 由部分至完全體重轉移 Partial to full weight transfer                 | 依耐受度 progressing as tolerated           |

### 殘肢塑型 **Residual Limb Shaping**

理想殘肢形態應為錐形（tapered/conical shape），有利於義肢套筒的懸掛與承重：

An ideal residual limb should be conical/tapered in shape, facilitating prosthetic socket suspension and weight-bearing:

- 避免圓柱形（cylindrical）或球根形（bulbous）殘肢 Avoid cylindrical or bulbous residual limbs
- 繃帶包紮應由遠端開始，壓力由遠端向近端遞減 Bandaging should begin distally with distal-to-proximal pressure gradient
- 教導個案正確的自我包紮技術 Teach proper self-bandaging technique
- 考慮使用夜間加壓套筒 Consider nighttime compression wraps

### 水腫管理 **Edema Management**

| 介入方式 Intervention                      | 說明 Description                                        | 適應症 Indications                   |
| :----------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------ | :----------------------------------- |
| **加壓繃帶 Compression bandaging**         | 多層彈性繃帶系統 Multi-layer elastic bandage system     | 術後初期 Early post-operative period |
| **矽膠套筒 Silicone liner**                | 定制加壓矯具 Custom compression orthosis                | 慢性水腫 Chronic edema               |
| **氣壓治療 Pneumatic therapy**             | 循序加壓裝置 Sequential compression device              | 水腫嚴重 Severe edema                |
| **徒手淋巴引流 Manual lymphatic drainage** | 輕柔的淋巴引流手法 Gentle lymphatic drainage techniques | 淋巴水腫 Lymphedema                  |
| **抬高 Elevation**                         | 殘肢高於心臟水平 Residual limb above heart level        | 急性腫脹 Acute swelling              |

## 功能評估 **Functional Assessment**

### 下肢 K 水平分級 **Lower Limb K-Levels**

K 水平（Medicare Functional Classification Level）是評估下肢截肢者義肢使用功能標準化的工具，對義肢報銷與治療目標設定至關重要。

K-levels (Medicare Functional Classification Level) is a standardized tool for assessing lower limb amputee prosthetic functional status, critical for prosthetic reimbursement and treatment goal-setting.

| K 水平 K-Level | 功能描述 Functional Description                                                                    | 義肢類型 Prosthesis Type                                           | 治療目標 Treatment Goals                                                 |
| :------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **K0**         | 無義肢使用能力，或因健康因素限制無法行走 Cannot use prosthesis or limited by health                | 標準配置或無義肢 Standard setup or no prosthesis                   | 照護者訓練、輪椅獨立 Caregiver training, wheelchair independence         |
| **K1**         | 家庭內及短距離平坦路面行走 Household and short-distance flat-surface ambulation                    | 基本機械膝 Basic mechanical knee (TF)；標準足部 Standard foot (TT) | 室內步行、平行桿內訓練 Indoor walking, parallel bar training             |
| **K2**         | 社區步行能力，可應對低速不平坦路面 Community ambulation, low-speed irregular surfaces              | 中階義肢組件 Mid-range components                                  | 社區步行、低速不平坦地面 Community ambulation, low-speed uneven terrain  |
| **K3**         | 多數環境下行走，可有快速步速需求 Most environments, potential for faster speeds                    | 高階義肢組件 High-performance components                           | 快速步行、樓梯、不平坦地形 Fast walking, stairs, uneven terrain          |
| **K4**         | 高體能需求活動：跑步、運動、職業需求 High-demand activities: running, sports, occupational demands | 競技/運動型義肢 Athletic/sport-specific prosthesis                 | 運動、跑步、高體能職業 Sports, running, physically demanding occupations |

Upper limb amputees use different functional classification systems, primarily based on prosthesis usage and hand function:

| **ABILHAND**                                              | 上肢日常活動能力量表 Upper limb daily activity capacity scale      |

## 第一階段：術後照護 **Phase 1: Post-surgical Management (0-4 Weeks)**

::: warning ⚠️ 此階段重點 Key Focus
傷口保護優先，漸進式活動以預防併發症。術後早期復健可顯著改善後續功能預後。
Wound protection is paramount. Progressive activity prevents complications. Early post-surgical rehabilitation significantly improves subsequent functional outcomes.
:::

### 治療目標 **Therapeutic Goals**

1. 促進傷口癒合，預防感染 Promote wound healing, prevent infection
2. 控制水腫 Manage edema
3. 維持保留關節之 ROM Maintain ROM of preserved joints
4. 預防攣縮（特別是髖屈曲與膝屈曲）Prevent contractures (especially hip flexion and knee flexion)
5. 疼痛控制 Pain management
6. 心理支持 Psychological support

### 干預策略 **Interventions**

| 介入 Intervention                    | 詳細內容 Details                                                          | 頻率 Frequency                                                       |
| :----------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **殘肢抬高 Residual limb elevation** | 保持殘肢高於心臟水平 Maintain above heart level                           | 20-30 分鐘/次，每日多次 20-30 min/session, multiple daily            |
| **冰敷 Ice application**             | 薄布隔離後冰敷 15-20 分鐘 Apply ice with barrier for 15-20 min            | 急性期每日 3-4 次 Acute phase 3-4x/day                               |
| **輕柔 ROM Gentle ROM**              | 所有關節之 PROM + AROM 所有方向 PROM + AROM all directions for all joints | 每日 2-3 回合 Each set 2-3x daily                                    |
| **等長收縮 Isometrics**              | 保留肌肉之輕柔等長收縮 Gentle isometrics of preserved muscles             | 每日 3-4 回合 Daily 3-4 sets                                         |
| **呼吸運動 Breathing exercises**     | 深呼吸、咳嗽訓練 Deep breathing, cough training                           | 每小時 5-10 次 5-10x/hour                                            |
| **床上轉位 Bed mobility**            | 安全翻身、側躺技巧 Safe turning, side-lying techniques                    | 日常活動中練習 Practice during daily activities                      |
| **殘肢繃帶教育 Bandaging education** | 教導正確繃帶包紮與移除 Teach proper bandaging application/removal         | 由治療師示範後個案練習 Therapist demonstration then patient practice |

### 攣縮預防要點 **Contracture Prevention**

| 截肢類型 Amputation | 易發生攣縮部位 Contracture Risk Site                         | 預防策略 Prevention Strategy                                             |
| :------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **經股截肢 TF**     | 髖屈曲、外展、外旋 Hip flexion, abduction, external rotation | 俯俯臥位、髖伸展運動 Prone positioning, hip extension exercises          |
| **經踝截肢 TT**     | 膝屈曲 Knee flexion                                          | 膝伸展運動、膝下加壓墊 Knee extension exercises, knee extension bolster  |
| **經肱截肢 TH**     | 肩屈曲、外展不足 Insufficient shoulder flexion/abduction     | 肩部全關節活動、爬牆運動 Shoulder PROM, wall climbing exercises          |
| **經腕截肢 TR**     | 肘屈曲 Elbow flexion                                         | 肘伸展運動、旋前旋後練習 Elbow extension exercises, pronation/supination |

### 疼痛管理 **Pain Management**

- **殘肢痛 Residual limb pain**：使用藥物（NSAIDs、鴉片類短期使用）、物理因子治療（冷療、電刺激）
  - Pharmacological (NSAIDs, short-term opioids), physical agents (cryotherapy, electrical stimulation)
- **幻肢痛 Phantom limb pain**：見[幻肢痛管理](#幻肢痛管理-phantom-limb-pain-management)章節
  - See [Phantom Limb Pain Management](#幻肢痛管理-phantom-limb-pain-management)
- **神經痛 Neuropathic pain**：藥物調整（gabapentin、pregabalin）、TENS
  - Medication adjustment (gabapentin, pregabalin), TENS

### 心理社會考量 **Psychosocial Considerations**

截肢是一種重大身體形象改變，可能引發哀傷反應、抑鬱、焦慮。治療師應：

Amputation represents a significant body image change that may trigger grief, depression, and anxiety. Therapists should:

- 以同理心傾聽，允許哀傷過程 Listen empathetically, allow the grieving process
- 提供實際功能資訊 Provide practical functional information
- 必要時轉介心理衛生團隊 Refer to mental health team when needed
- 鼓勵家屬參與復健 Encourage family participation in rehabilitation
- 連結支持團體與同儕資源 Connect to support groups and peer resources

## 第二階段：義肢準備 **Phase 2: Pre-prosthetic Training (4-8 Weeks)**

### 治療目標 **Therapeutic Goals**

1. 強化殘肢與核心肌群 Strengthen residual limb and core muscles
2. 開始站立平衡訓練 Initiate standing balance training
3. 提升功能性 ADL 能力 Improve functional ADL capacity
4. 為義肢穿戴做準備 Prepare for prosthetic fitting
5. 持續水腫管理 Continue edema management

### 干預策略 **Interventions**

| 介入 Intervention                        | 詳細內容 Details                                                 | 進階 Progression                                  |
| :--------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------ |
| **殘肢強化 Residual limb strengthening** | 開放鏈與封閉鏈肌力訓練 Open & closed kinetic chain strengthening | 阻力漸增 Resistance progression                   |
| **核心穩定 Core stability**              | 平板支撐、死蟲式、鳥狗式 Plank, dead bug, bird dog               | 由穩定面至不穩定面 Stable to unstable surfaces    |
| **坐姿平衡 Sitting balance**             | 靜態→動態坐姿平衡 Static→dynamic sitting balance                 | 雙手釋放、軀幹偏移 Hands-free, trunk perturbation |
| **站立訓練 Standing training**           | 平行桿內站立、重心轉移 Parallel bar standing, weight shifting    | 單腳站立（健側）Single-leg stance (sound side)    |
| **轉位訓練 Transfer training**           | 床↔輪椅、輪椅↔馬桶 Bed↔wheelchair, wheelchair↔toilet         | 減少輔具需求 Reduce assistive device needs        |
| **ADL 訓練 ADL training**                | 穿衣、如厕、衛浴 Dressing, toileting, bathing                    | 提升獨立程度 Increase independence                |

## 第三階段：義肢訓練 **Phase 3: Prosthetic Training (8-16 Weeks)**

::: warning ⚠️ 義肢穿戴警語 Prosthetic Wearing Precautions
初次穿戴義肢應由義肢技師與治療師共同監督。每次穿戴時間應由短漸增（最初 30 分鐘→逐步增加至全天），並密切監測皮膚完整性。
Initial prosthetic wear should be supervised by both prosthetist and therapist. Wear time should progressively increase (initial 30 min → gradual increase to full day) with close monitoring of skin integrity.
:::

### 治療目標 **Therapeutic Goals**

1. 學習義肢穿脫技術 Learn prosthetic donning/doffing techniques
2. 達成安全、有效的義肢步行 Achieve safe and efficient prosthetic gait
3. 提升義肢使用下之 ADL 功能 Improve ADL performance with prosthesis
4. 建立步態對稱性 Establish gait symmetry
5. 增加穿戴耐受時間 Increase wear tolerance duration

### 穿脫訓練 **Donning/Doffing Training**

| 義肢類型 Prosthesis Type                      | 穿戴方法 Donning Method                                                        | 注意事項 Precautions                                      |
| :-------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------- |
| **吸著式懸掛（下肢）Suction suspension (LL)** | 殘肢塗滑石粉→穿襪→套入→釋放繃帶 Apply powder → sock → insert → release bandage | 確保無皺褶 Ensure no wrinkles                             |
| **矽膠套筒+鎖 pin lock (TT)**                 | 矽膠套筒穿著→鎖入管柱 Liner roll on → pin lock insertion                       | 鎖入機制確實接合 Verify locking mechanism engagement      |
| **肌電義肢 Myoelectric**                      | 穿戴→電極校準 Donning → electrode calibration                                  | 皮膚清潔、電極接觸良好 Clean skin, good electrode contact |
| **肩背懸掛（上肢）Shoulder-suspended (UL)**   | 肩帶→手臂套入→調整帶長 Shoulder harness → arm insert → strap adjustment        | 不過度拉扯肩部 Avoid excessive shoulder tension           |

### 步態訓練進階 **Gait Training Progression**

#### 階段一：平行桿內 **Stage 1: Parallel Bars**

## 幻肢痛管理 **Phantom Limb Pain Management**

幻肢感（phantom sensation）是指截肢者感受到已不存在之肢體的感覺，發生率高達 90% 以上。幻肢痛（phantom limb pain）則是發生在幻肢部位的疼痛感覺，約 50-80% 的截肢者會經歷，其中約 10-15% 為嚴重慢性疼痛。

Phantom sensation refers to sensations perceived in a limb that no longer exists, occurring in over 90% of amputees. Phantom limb pain is pain perceived in the phantom limb region, affecting 50-80% of amputees, with 10-15% experiencing severe chronic pain.

### 機制 **Mechanisms**

- **周邊機制 Peripheral**：神經瘤形成、異位放電 Neuroma formation, ectopic discharge
- **脊髓機制 Spinal**：背角重塑、失傳入性增強 Dorsal horn remodeling, deafferentation enhancement
- **皮質機制 Cortical**：皮質重塑、感覺運動地圖失序 Cortical reorganization, sensorimotor map disruption

### 治療策略 **Treatment Strategies**

| 策略 Strategy                                 | 說明 Description                                                                               | 證據等級 Evidence Level     |
| :-------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| **鏡像治療 Mirror therapy**                   | 使用鏡子創造健側肢體運動的視覺錯覺 Creates visual illusion of sound limb movement using mirror | 強 Strong (Cochrane review) |
| **TENS**                                      | 經皮電神經刺激，調控疼痛門控 Transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation, gate control         | 中 Moderate                 |
| **漸進式運動想像 Graded motor imagery (GMI)** | 左右辨識→運動想像→鏡像治療 Laterality recognition → motor imagery → mirror therapy             | 中 Moderate                 |
| **虛擬實境 VR therapy**                       | 沉浸式虛擬環境中的肢體運動訓練 Immersive virtual environment limb movement training            | 中 Moderate (emerging)      |
| **加壓治療 Compression therapy**              | 殘肢加壓套筒 Residual limb compression wrapping                                                | 低-中 Low-moderate          |
| **生物回饋 Biofeedback**                      | 肌電回饋訓練殘肢肌肉 EMG feedback for residual limb muscle training                            | 低-中 Low-moderate          |
| **藥物治療 Pharmacotherapy**                  | Gabapentin, pregabalin, duloxetine, tramadol                                                   | 中-強 Moderate-strong       |

### 鏡像治療方案 **Mirror Therapy Protocol**

鏡像治療是處理幻肢痛的循證首選非藥物介入：

Mirror therapy is the evidence-based first-line non-pharmacological intervention for phantom limb pain:

1. **設定 Setup**：個案坐於鏡箱前，將殘肢與鏡面同側放置
   - Patient seated before mirror box, residual limb placed on mirror side
2. **健側運動 Sound limb exercise**：在鏡中看到健側肢體的反射影像
   - Observe reflection of sound limb in mirror
3. **執行動作 Perform movements**：彎曲、伸直、握拳、張開手指等
   - Flexion, extension, fist making, finger spreading, etc.
4. **專注想像 Focused imagery**：想像幻肢正在執行相同動作
   - Imagine phantom limb performing same movements
5. **頻率 Frequency**：每次 15-20 分鐘，每日 2-3 次
   - 15-20 minutes per session, 2-3 times daily
6. **療程 Duration**：至少 4-12 週至少為一完整療程
   - Minimum 4-12 weeks for complete course

::: warning ⚠️ 鏡像治療禁忌 Mirror Therapy Contraindications
鏡像治療不適用於：嚴重認知障礙、無法維持注意力、嚴重幻覺或精神症狀之個案。開始前應排除中風後忽略症（若為單側）。
Mirror therapy is contraindicated in: severe cognitive impairment, inability to maintain attention, severe hallucinations or psychosis. Hemispatial neglect (if unilateral) should be ruled out before initiation.
:::

## 注意事項 **Precautions**

### 皮膚完整性 **Skin Integrity**

::: warning ⚠️ 皮膚警語 Skin Alert
義肢穿戴者之殘肢皮膚損傷是最常見的併發症之一。任何皮膚發紅若持續 30 分鐘以上未消退，應立即脫除義肢並評估。
Skin breakdown on the residual limb is one of the most common complications. Any redness persisting beyond 30 minutes after prosthesis removal warrants immediate discontinuation and assessment.
:::

| 風險因素 Risk Factor                        | 預防策略 Prevention                                                                  |
| :------------------------------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **繃帶/套筒褶皺 Wrinkles in bandage/liner** | 穿戴前仔細檢查；正確穿著技巧 Inspect carefully before donning; proper technique      |
| **過度出汗 Excessive sweating**             | 吸汗襪、保持皮膚清潔、使用吸濕粉 Moisture-wicking socks, skin hygiene, drying powder |
| **毛囊炎 Folliculitis**                     | 每日殘肢清洗、透氣時間 Daily residual limb washing, air time                         |
| **壓力點 Pressure points**                  | 定期義肢對線評估 Regular prosthetic alignment assessment                             |
| **骨突處突出 Bony prominence**              | 套筒內襯加墊 Pad socket liner over bony prominences                                  |

### 幻肢痛注意事項 **Phantom Pain Precautions**

- 幻肢痛急性發作時，避免過度藥物依賴 Avoid medication dependency during acute phantom pain episodes
- 漸進式暴露結合認知行為策略 Combining graded exposure with cognitive behavioral strategies
- 記錄疼痛日誌以辨識觸發因子 Maintain pain diaries to identify triggers
- 嚴重頑固性疼痛應轉介疼痛專科 Severe refractory pain should be referred to pain specialist
- 營養狀態（維生素 B12、蛋白質）影響神經修復 Nutritional status (B12, protein) affects nerve recovery

### 對側肢體照護 **Contralateral Limb Care**

::: warning ⚠️ 對側風險 Contralateral Risk
下肢截肢者的對側肢體承受更高負荷與跌倒風險。對側肢體的任何傷口或問題可能完全剝奪個案的行走能力。
The contralateral limb of lower limb amputees bears increased load and fall risk. Any injury or problem to the contralateral limb may completely eliminate the patient's ambulation capacity.
:::

| 風險 Risk                                     | 預防策略 Prevention                                                                              |
| :-------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **承重增加 Overloading**                      | 義肢對線優化、輔具使用 Prosthesis alignment optimization, assistive device use                   |
| **足部潰瘍 Foot ulceration**                  | 定期足部檢查、適當鞋具 Regular foot checks, appropriate footwear                                 |
| **跌倒傷害 Fall injuries**                    | 平衡訓練、環境改造、照明改善 Balance training, environmental modification, lighting improvement  |
| **退化性關節炎 Degenerative joint disease**   | 體重管理、低衝擊運動、肌力維持 Weight management, low-impact exercise, strength maintenance      |
| **血管疾病進展 Vascular disease progression** | 血糖控制、戒菸、定期血管評估 Blood sugar control, smoking cessation, regular vascular assessment |

### 跌倒預防 **Fall Prevention**

跌倒是截肢者面臨的主要風險之一，特別是在義肢使用初期：

Falls are a major risk for amputees, especially during early prosthetic use:

- 訓練跌倒安全策略（前倒、側倒）Train safe fall strategies (forward fall, lateral fall)
- 教導地面起立技巧 Teach floor rising techniques
- 家庭環境評估（消除絆倒 hazard、改善照明）Home environment assessment (remove trip hazards, improve lighting)
- 穿戴穩固鞋具 Wear stable footwear
- 避免光滑地面、潮濕環境 Avoid slippery floors, wet environments

### 術後 0-4 週 **Post-operative Weeks 0-4**

- [ ] 呼吸運動 Breathing exercises
