# 淋巴水腫復健 **Lymphedema Rehabilitation**

## 概述 Overview

淋巴水腫是一種因淋巴系統功能障礙導致富含蛋白質的液體在組織間隙中異常積聚的慢性疾病。Lymphedema is a chronic condition characterized by the pathological accumulation of protein-rich fluid in the interstitial space due to dysfunction of the lymphatic system.

### 原發性與繼發性淋巴水腫 Primary vs Secondary Lymphedema

| 類型 Type        | 定義 Definition                                       | 常見原因 Common Causes                                                                                                                                 |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 原發性 Primary   | 先天性淋巴系統發育異常 Congenital lymphatic dysplasia | 先天性淋巴水腫（如Milroy病、Meige病）、淋巴管發育不全或增生 Congenital lymphedema (Milroy disease, Meige disease), lymphatic hypoplasia or hyperplasia |
| 繼發性 Secondary | 後天因素導致淋巴系統損傷 Acquired lymphatic damage    | 癌症手術/放射治療、絲蟲病、創傷、感染 Cancer surgery/radiation, filariasis, trauma, infection                                                          |

原發性淋巴水腫較少見，約占所有病例的10%，通常在青春期前發病。Secondary lymphedema占大多數，最常見於癌症治療之後。Primary lymphedema is less common, accounting for approximately 10% of all cases, typically presenting before adolescence. Secondary lymphedema accounts for the majority of cases, most commonly following cancer treatment.

### 病理生理學 Pathophysiology

淋巴水腫的發展涉及以下機制：

1. **淋巴輸出量減少 Reduced lymph transport** — 淋巴管損傷或缺失導致液體回流受阻 Impaired fluid return due to damaged or absent lymphatic vessels
2. **組織間液蛋白濃度升高 Elevated interstitial protein concentration** — 蛋白質積聚增加組織滲透壓，吸引更多液體 Protein accumulation increases tissue oncotic pressure, drawing more fluid
3. **慢性發炎 Chronic inflammation** — 蛋白質觸發纖維母細胞增殖與脂肪沉積 Protein triggers fibroblast proliferation and adipose deposition
4. **組織纖維化 Tissue fibrosis** — 晚期出現不可逆的組織硬化 Irreversible tissue induration in advanced stages

::: warning 重要觀念
淋巴水腫是一種進行性疾病，若不治療會持續惡化。早期介入可顯著改善預後。Lymphedema is a progressive condition that worsens without intervention. Early management significantly improves outcomes.
:::

### 國際淋巴學會分期 ISL Staging

| 分期 Stage              | 描述 Description  | 臨床特徵 Clinical Features                                                                                                              |
| ----------------------- | ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Stage 0（亞臨床期）** | Subclinical stage | 淋巴運輸功能受損但無可見水腫 Impaired lymph transport without visible edema; may have heaviness or tingling 可能有沉重感或刺痛感        |
| **Stage I（早期）**     | Early stage       | 可凹性水腫，抬高可改善 Pitting edema, improves with elevation 組織柔軟，壓痕後緩慢回彈 Soft tissue, pit sign positive, rebounds slowly  |
| **Stage II（中期）**    | Late stage        | 抬高無法完全改善水腫 Edema not fully reversible with elevation 組織逐漸硬化，壓痕不明顯 Tissue becomes firmer, pitting less evident     |
| **Stage III（晚期）**   | Advanced stage    | 皮膚增厚、角化、疣狀增生 Skin thickening, keratosis, wart-like growths（象皮病 elephantiasis）不可凹性硬腫 Non-pitting, hard induration |

::: warning 重要區分
Stage II的組織纖維化程度可影響治療策略。Stage II with significant fibrosis may require more aggressive decongestion approaches compared to Stage II with predominantly fluid accumulation.
:::

## 常見病因 Common Causes

### 乳癌術後淋巴水腫 Post-Breast Cancer Surgery Lymphedema

乳癌相關的上肢淋巴水腫是繼發性淋巴水腫最常見的原因。上肢淋巴水腫的發生率因手術方式而異：

| 手術類型 Surgical Procedure                               | 淋巴水腫發生率 Incidence Rate |
| --------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| 腋下淋巴結切除術（ALND）Axillary lymph node dissection    | 20–40%                        |
| 前哨淋巴結活檢（SLNB）Sentinel lymph node biopsy          | 5–15%                         |
| 乳房切除術+腋下手術 Mastectomy + axillary surgery         | 15–30%                        |
| 乳房保留術+放射治療 Breast-conserving surgery + radiation | 5–10%                         |

放射治療會進一步增加淋巴水腫風險，因為放射線可造成淋巴管纖維化。Radiation therapy further increases lymphedema risk as radiation can cause lymphatic fibrosis.

### 婦科癌症術後淋巴水腫 Post-Gynecological Cancer Lymphedema

子宮頸癌、子宮內膜癌和卵巢癌的治療（包括骨盆腔淋巴結切除術和放射治療）可導致下肢淋巴水腫。治療方式包括骨盆腔淋巴結切除術及放射治療，可導致下肢淋巴水腫。

下肢淋巴水腫在婦科癌症後的發生率約為10–35%。Lower extremity lymphedema occurs in approximately 10–35% of patients following gynecological cancer treatment.

### 絲蟲病 Filariasis

絲蟲病是全球淋巴水腫最常見的原因，特別在熱帶和亞熱帶地區。Filariasis is the most common cause of lymphedema worldwide, particularly in tropical and sub-tropical regions.

- 由蚊子傳播的班氏絲蟲（_Wuchereria bancrofti_）引起 Caused by _Wuchereria bancrofti_, transmitted by mosquitoes
- 成蟲寄生於淋巴管，造成慢性阻塞與發炎 Adult worms inhabit lymphatic vessels, causing chronic obstruction and inflammation
- 可導致嚴重的象皮病 Can lead to severe elephantiasis
- 全球約有1.2億人受影響 Approximately 120 million people affected globally

### 先天性淋巴水腫 Congenital Lymphedema

- **Milroy病**：家族性早發性下肢淋巴水腫，通常在出生時或兩歲前出現 Milroy disease: familial early-onset lower limb lymphedema, typically present at birth or before age 2
- **Meige病（原發性淋巴水腫伴黃指甲）**：在青春期發病 Meige disease (primary lymphedema with yellow nails): onset at puberty
- **淋巴水腫-雙行睫症候群**：伴隨眉毛異常 Lymphedema-distichiasis syndrome: associated with abnormal eyelashes

## 評估 Assessment

<details>
<summary>圓周測量 Circumferential Measurement</summary>

使用柔性量尺在標準解剖標記點測量肢體周長，是最基本且廣泛使用的評估方法。Use a flexible tape measure at standard anatomical landmarks; this is the most fundamental and widely used assessment method.

**標準測量點 Standard Measurement Points：**

上肢 Upper Extremity：

- 指間關節（掌指關節）間隙 Web space between digits (metacarpophalangeal joints)
- 掌指關節 Metacarpophalangeal joints
- 腕關節（橈骨莖突水平）Wrist (radial styloid level)
- 前臂中點 Mid-forearm
- 肘關節（肱骨內外上髁）Elbow (epicondyles)
- 上臂中點 Mid-upper arm
- 腋窩頂 Axillary fold

下肢 Lower Extremity：

- 趾蹼 Web space of toes
- 跗跖關節 Metatarsophalangeal joints
- 踝關節（內外踝水平）Ankle (malleolar level)
- 小腿中點 Mid-calf
- 膝關節（髕骨中點）Knee (mid-patella)
- 大腿中點 Mid-thigh
- 會陰水平 Perineal level

::: warning 測量注意
每次測量應使用相同的解剖標記點，並由同一測量者操作以確保可靠性。Make measurements at the same anatomical landmarks each time, performed by the same examiner to ensure reliability.
:::

</details>

<details>
<summary>體積測量 Volume Measurement</summary>

體積測量被認為是測量肢體水腫的「金標準」。Volume measurement is considered the "gold standard" for limb edema assessment.

- **排水法 Water displacement method**：將肢體浸入裝滿水的容器中，收集溢出水量 Immersing the limb in a water-filled container and collecting displaced water
- **截頭圓錐公式 Frustum of a cone formula**：

$$V = \sum \frac{h}{3\pi}(C_1^2 + C_1C_2 + C_2^2)$$

其中 $h$ 為相鄰測量點之間的距離，$C_1$ 和 $C_2$ 為相鄰圓周 Where $h$ is the distance between adjacent measurement points, and $C_1$ and $C_2$ are adjacent circumferences.

### 生物電阻抗分析 Bioimpedance Analysis (BIA)

生物電阻抗是一種非侵入性的測量方法，可檢測組織中的液體含量變化。Bioimpedance is a non-invasive method that detects changes in tissue fluid content.

| 優點 Advantages                                   | 缺點 Limitations                                    |
| ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| 客觀且可重複 Objective and reproducible           | 設備成本較高 Higher equipment cost                  |
| 可偵測亞臨床水腫 Can detect subclinical edema     | 受皮膚乾燥影響 Affected by skin dryness             |
| 無需接觸患部 No contact with affected area needed | 需要標準化操作條件 Requires standardized conditions |

### 肢體體積計算 Limb Volume Calculation

肢體體積百分比差異（Limb Volume Difference, LVD）的計算：

$$LVD = \frac{患側體積 - 健側體積}{健側體積} \times 100\%$$

- **差異 < 10%**：可能為正常變異或輕度水腫 Likely normal variation or mild edema
- **差異 10–30%**：中度淋巴水腫 Moderate lymphedema
- **差異 > 30%**：重度淋巴水腫 Severe lymphedema

::: warning 體積差異
兩側肢體體積差異超過10%通常被認為具有臨床意義。A difference of more than 10% between limbs is generally considered clinically significant.
:::

</details>

## 複合式消腫治療 Complex Decongestive Therapy (CDT)

複合式消腫治療是淋巴水腫治療的「金標準」，包含兩個主要階段。Complex Decongestive Therapy (CDT) is the "gold standard" for lymphedema management, consisting of two major phases.

<details>
<summary>第一階段：消腫期 Phase I — Reduction Phase（2–4 週）</summary>

此階段目標為最大限度地減少肢體體積，通常在治療中心進行密集治療。This phase aims to maximize limb volume reduction with intensive treatment at a clinic.

#### 手動淋巴引流 Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD)

MLD是一種輕柔的、有節奏的手法治療，旨在促進淋巴液流向功能正常的淋巴引流區域。MLD is a gentle, rhythmic manual technique designed to redirect lymphatic flow toward functioning lymphatic drainage territories.

**MLD手法原則 Principles of MLD Technique：**

1. **近端至遠端 Proximal-to-distal approach**
   - 先處理軀幹和近端淋巴結區域，再處理遠端肢體 Treat the trunk and proximal nodal regions first, then the distal limb
   - 顺序：頸部→鎖骨上→腋窩→肩部→軀幹→上肢/下肢 Sequence: neck → supraclavicular → axilla → shoulder → trunk → upper/lower limb

2. **方向 Direction of strokes**
   - 從水腫區域朝向功能正常的淋巴結方向推送 Stroke from the edematous area toward functioning lymph nodes
   - 每個推stroke約3–4秒 Each stroke lasts approximately 3–4 seconds

3. **壓力 Pressure**
   - 使用約30–40 mmHg的輕柔壓力 Gentle pressure of approximately 30–40 mmHg
   - 應足以拉伸皮膚但不造成疼痛 Sufficient to stretch the skin without causing pain

4. **皮膚伸展 Skin stretch**
   - 施術者的手掌完全貼合皮膚，進行固定伸展後向前推進 Handpads maintain full contact with skin, applying stationary stretch then advancing forward

::: warning MLD 禁忌症

- 急性蜂窩織炎 Acute cellulitis
- 深部靜脈血栓形成（DVT）Deep vein thrombosis
- 急性心臟衰竭 Acute cardiac failure
- 未控制的甲亢 Uncontrolled hyperthyroidism
  :::

#### 短彈性繃帶包紮 Short-Stretch Bandaging

短彈性繃帶是CDT的核心組件，提供高靜止壓和低工作壓。Short-stretch bandaging is a core component of CDT, providing high resting pressure and low working pressure.

**包紮原則 Bandaging Principles：**

| 原則 Principle                      | 說明 Description                                                           |
| ----------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 壓力梯度 Pressure gradient          | 從遠端至近端遞減 Graduated from distal to proximal                         |
| 壓力類別 Compression class          | 通常使用30–40 mmHg（Stage II-III） Usually 30–40 mmHg (Stage II-III)       |
| 墊襯 Padding                        | 使用棉墊或泡沫墊分散壓力 Use cotton or foam padding to distribute pressure |
| 錐形形狀 Conical shape              | 沿著肢體的自然錐形進行包紮 Follow the natural conical shape of the limb    |
| 骨突保護 Bony prominence protection | 在骨突處增加額外墊襯 Add extra padding over bony prominences               |
| 指/趾暴露 Fingers/toes exposed      | 保持指尖暴露以監測血液循環 Keep fingertips exposed to monitor circulation  |

**繃帶選擇 Bandage Selection：**

| 繃帶類型 Bandage Type                           | 壓力範圍 Pressure Range | 適用期適用期 Phase |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------ |
| 短彈性繃帶（棉質）Short-stretch (cotton)        | 20–40 mmHg              | Phase I & II       |
| 短彈性繃帶（合成纖維）Short-stretch (synthetic) | 20–40 mmHg              | Phase I & II       |
| 多層繃帶系統 Multi-layer bandage system         | 30–40 mmHg              | Phase I            |

::: warning 包紮注意
確保壓力均勻分佈，避免出現「止血帶效應」。繃帶不應造成疼痛、麻木、變色或皮膚溫度改變。Ensure even pressure distribution and avoid a "tourniquet effect." Bandages should not cause pain, numbness, discoloration, or skin temperature changes.
:::

#### 皮膚護理 Skin Care

淋巴水腫患者的皮膚護理至關重要，因為受損的皮膚容易發生感染。Skin care is critical for lymphedema patients as compromised skin is prone to infection.

- **清潔 Cleansing**：每日溫和清洗，避免鹼性肥皂 Use mild cleansing daily, avoid alkaline soap
- **保濕 Moisturizing**：使用無香料的保濕乳液，每日至少兩次 Fragrance-free moisturizer, at least twice daily
- **指甲護理 Nail care**：保持指甲修剪整齊，避免甲溝炎 Keep nails trimmed neatly to avoid paronychia
- **避免損傷 Avoid trauma**：避免在患側測量血壓、抽血、注射 Avoid BP measurement, blood draws, injections on the affected side
- **防曬 Sun protection**：避免長時間日曬，使用防曬乳液 Avoid prolonged sun exposure, use sunscreen

#### 運動 Exercise

運動是CDT不可或缺的組件。Exercise is an integral component of CDT.

**運動原則 Exercise Principles：**

1. 在繃帶或壓力衣穿著下進行運動 Perform exercises with bandages or compression garments in place
2. 從簡單的關節活動度運動開始 Start with simple range of motion exercises
3. 逐漸增加強度和阻力 Progressively increase intensity and resistance
4. 注意呼吸配合 Maintain breathing coordination
5. 避免過度疲勞 Avoid overexertion

**上肢運動範例 Upper Extremity Exercise Examples：**

| 運動 Exercise                             | 目的 Purpose                                     | 重複次數 Repetitions |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | -------------------- |
| 手指屈曲伸展 Finger flexion/extension     | 促進手部淋巴回流 Promote hand lymphatic drainage | 10–15次              |
| 手腕旋轉 Wrist circles                    | 改善手腕關節活動度 Improve wrist ROM             | 10次雙向             |
| 前臂旋前旋後 Forearm pronation/supination | 維持前臂旋轉功能 Maintain forearm rotation       | 10次                 |
| 肩關節繞環 Shoulder circumduction         | 改善肩部活動度 Improve shoulder ROM              | 10次                 |
| 上肢抬舉 Arm elevation                    | 促進淋巴回流 Promote lymphatic return            | 保持10秒，重複10次   |

<details>
<summary>第二階段：維持期 Phase II — Maintenance Phase（終身）</summary>

維持期的目標是維持第一階段的治療效果，並防止水腫復發。The goal of Phase II is to maintain the gains from Phase I and prevent recurrence.

#### 壓力衣物 Compression Garments

壓力衣物是維持期的基礎治療。Compression garments form the foundation of maintenance therapy.

| 衣物類型 Garment Type                         | 壓力等級 Pressure Class | 適用情況 Indication                   |
| --------------------------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| 壓力手套/襪套 Compression glove/stocking)     | 15–20 mmHg (Class I)    | 輕度水腫，預防 Mild edema, prevention |
| 壓力手臂套/腿套 Compression sleeve/leg sleeve | 20–30 mmHg (Class II)   | 中度水腫 Moderate edema               |
| 壓力手臂套/腿套 Compression sleeve/leg sleeve | 30–40 mmHg (Class III)  | 中重度水腫 Moderate-severe edema      |
| 定制壓力衣物 Custom compression garments      | 40–60 mmHg (Class IV)   | 重度水腫 Severe edema                 |

**壓力衣物穿著指南 Compression Garment Donning Guidelines：**

1. 清潔皮膚並塗抹少量潤滑劑 Clean skin and apply a small amount of lubricant
2. 從指尖或腳趾開始，均勻拉至近端 Start from fingertips or toes, pulling evenly to proximal end
3. 確保無皺褶或捲邊 Ensure no wrinkles or rolled edges
4. 每日穿著，至少12小時/天 Wear daily, at least 12 hours/day

::: warning 壓力衣物注意
夜間是否穿著壓力衣物應由治療團隊個體化決定。有些患者夜間需要穿著更高壓力的壓力袖或壓力襪。Whether to wear compression garments at night should be individually determined by the treatment team. Some patients may require higher-pressure sleeves or stockings at night.
:::

#### 自我淋巴引流 Self-Manual Lymphatic Drainage (Self-MLD)

患者應接受自我MLD的教育和訓練，以便在維持期自行進行。Patients should be educated and trained in self-MLD for independent maintenance.

**自我MLD步驟 Self-MLD Steps：**

1. 從頸部開始，輕柔地將淋巴液推向鎖骨上窩 Start at the neck, gently pushing lymph toward the supraclavicular fossa
2. 重複每側頸部5–10次 Repeat 5–10 times per side of the neck
3. 移至患側腋窩，朝向鎖骨方向 Move to the affected axilla, directing toward the clavicle
4. 重複腋窩區域5–10次 Repeat 5–10 times in the axillary region
5. 最後進行患肢的引流 Finally, perform drainage of the affected limb
6. 每個區域重複5–10次，每日2–3次 Repeat each area 5–10 times, 2–3 times daily

#### 維持期運動 Maintenance Exercise

維持期運動應結合有氧運動和阻力訓練。Maintenance exercise should combine aerobic and resistance training.

| 運動類型 Exercise Type          | 範例 Examples                                                  | 頻率 Frequency                     |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| 有氧運動 Aerobic exercise       | 步行、游泳、騎自行車 Walking, swimming, cycling                | 3–5次/週，30分鐘 3–5x/week, 30 min |
| 阻力訓練 Resistance training    | 彈力帶、啞鈴、機械式訓練 Resistance bands, dumbbells, machines | 2–3次/週 2–3x/week                 |
| 柔軟度訓練 Flexibility training | 拉伸、瑜伽 Stretching, yoga                                    | 每日 Daily                         |
| 呼吸訓練 Breathing exercises    | 深呼吸、腹式呼吸 Deep breathing, diaphragmatic breathing       | 每日 Daily                         |

::: warning 運動注意
所有運動應在穿著壓力衣物的情況下進行。避免突然增加阻力或運動時間。如出現水腫增加、疼痛或不適，應停止運動並諮詢治療團隊。All exercises should be performed with compression garments on. Avoid sudden increases in resistance or duration. If increased swelling, pain, or discomfort occurs, stop exercise and consult the treatment team.
:::

## 手動淋巴引流技巧 Manual Lymphatic Drainage Technique

<details>
<summary>筆觸方向 Stroke Direction</summary>

| 區域 Area                      | 引流方向 Drainage Direction                                                      |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 頸部 Neck                      | 朝向鎖骨上窩 Toward supraclavicular fossa                                        |
| 鎖骨上窩 Supraclavicular fossa | 向下朝向胸導管或右淋巴導管 Downward toward thoracic duct or right lymphatic duct |
| 肩部 Shoulder                  | 朝向腋窩 Toward axilla                                                           |
| 上臂 Upper arm                 | 從遠端至近端，朝向腋窩 Distal to proximal, toward axilla                         |
| 前臂 Forearm                   | 從遠端至近端，朝向肘窩 Distal to proximal, toward antecubital fossa              |
| 手部 Hand                      | 從指尖朝向手腕 From fingertips toward wrist                                      |
| 躯幹 Trunk                     | 朝向腋窩和腹股溝 Toward axilla and groin                                         |
| 下肢 Lower limb                | 從遠端至近端，朝向腹股溝 Distal to proximal, toward inguinal region              |

### 近端至遠端原則 Proximal-to-Distal Approach

::: warning 引流順序
**永遠先處理近端區域，再處理遠端區域。** Always treat proximal regions before distal regions.

例如：在引流上肢之前，必須先處理頸部和腋窩區域。For example: before draining the upper limb, the neck and axillary regions must be treated first.

**原因 Reason：**如果先處理遠端區域而近端通路受阻，液體無法有效回流，可能加重水腫。If distal regions are treated first but proximal pathways are blocked, fluid cannot effectively return and edema may worsen.
:::

</details>

## 短彈性繃帶包紮 Short-Stretch Bandaging

<details>
<summary>繃帶種類與特性 Bandage Types and Properties</summary>

| 特性 Property           | 短彈性繃帶 Short-Stretch | 長彈性繃帶 Long-Stretch |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------------- |
| 靜止壓 Resting pressure | 高 High                  | 低 Low                  |
| 工作壓 Working pressure | 低 Low                   | 高 High                 |
| 適用於 lymphedema       | 首選 First choice        | 不推薦 Not recommended  |
| 舒適度 Comfort          | 較好 Better              | 較差 Worse              |
| 穿脫 Ease of use        | 需要技巧 Requires skill  | 容易 Easy               |

::: warning 繃帶選擇
長彈性繃帶（如彈性繃帶 elastic bandage）不適合用於淋巴水腫治療，因為其高工作壓可能在肌肉收縮時造成壓力過高，而靜止壓不足。Long-stretch bandages (e.g., elastic bandages) are not suitable for lymphedema treatment because their high working pressure may cause excessive pressure during muscle contraction while providing insufficient resting pressure.
:::

### 包紮技術 Bandaging Technique

**Step 1：襪套保護 Stocking liner protection**

- 先穿著襪套（如Tubigrip或棉質襪套）以保護皮膚 Put on a stocking liner (e.g., Tubigrip or cotton liner) to protect the skin

**Step 2：遠端固定錨點 Distal anchor**

- 從趾蹼或指間開始，以8字形包紮固定遠端 Start with a figure-eight at the web space to anchor distally

**Step 3：螺旋包紮 Spiral wrapping**

- 從遠端至近端以螺旋方式包紮每圈重疊50% Wrap spirally from distal to proximal, each turn overlapping 50%

**Step 4：壓力梯度 Pressure gradient**

- 確保遠端壓力最高，近端壓力最低 Ensure highest pressure distally, lowest proximally

**Step 5：8字形包紮 Figure-eight wrapping**

- 在關節處（手腕、手肘、膝蓋）使用8字形技術 Use figure-eight technique at joints (wrist, elbow, knee)

**Step 6：收尾固定 Final fixation**

- 以膠帶或固定繃帶固定末端 Secure the end with tape or fixation bandage

::: warning 壓力監測
使用壓力監測設備（如Futuro壓力監測器）確認壓力分布正確。正常壓力範圍應在20–40 mmHg之間。Use pressure monitoring devices (e.g., Futuro pressure monitor) to confirm correct pressure distribution. Normal pressure range should be 20–40 mmHg.
:::

</details>

## 運動原則 Exercise Principles

<details>
<summary>運動與水腫的關係 Exercise and Edema</summary>

- 運動時肌肉收縮可促進淋巴液回流 Muscle contraction during exercise promotes lymphatic return
- 繃帶或壓力衣提供反作用力，增強肌肉泵功能 Bandages or compression garments provide resistance, enhancing the muscle pump
- 運動應在壓力衣物穿著下進行 Exercise should be performed with compression garments

### 活動範圍運動 Active Range of Motion (ROM)

**上肢 active ROM Upper Extremity ROM Exercises：**

| 運動 Exercise                             | 關節 Joint                 | 方向 Direction                                            |
| ----------------------------------------- | -------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| 手指屈曲伸展 Finger flexion/extension     | 掌指關節 MCP joints        | 屈曲/伸展 Flexion/extension                               |
| 手腕屈曲伸展 Wrist flexion/extension      | 腕關節 Wrist joint         | 屈曲/伸展 Flexion/extension                               |
| 前臂旋前旋後 Forearm pronation/supination | 橈尺關節 Radioulnar joints | 旋前/旋後 Pronation/supination                            |
| 手肘屈曲伸展 Elbow flexion/extension      | 肘關節 Elbow joint         | 屈曲/伸展 Flexion/extension                               |
| 肩關節活動 Shoulder movements             | 肩關節 Shoulder joint      | 外展/內收/屈曲/伸展 Abduction/adduction/flexion/extension |

### 帶阻力的 Resistance Exercise with Compression

- 從低阻力開始（彈力帶、輕啞鈴） Start with low resistance (resistance bands, light dumbbells)
- 逐漸增加阻力和重複次數 Progressively increase resistance and repetitions
- 穿著壓力衣物進行 Wear compression garments during exercise
- 注意呼吸，避免Valsalva動作 Maintain breathing, avoid Valsalva maneuver

### 游泳 Swimming

游泳是淋巴水腫患者理想的運動選擇。Swimming is an ideal exercise for lymphedema patients.

**游泳的好處 Benefits of Swimming：**

- 水壓提供全身性的壓力 Water pressure provides whole-body compression
- 全身性運動，促進淋巴回流 Whole-body movement promotes lymphatic return
- 低衝擊性，關節負荷小 Low impact, minimal joint loading
- 改善心肺功能 Improves cardiovascular fitness

**游泳注意事項 Swimming Precautions：**

- 穿著壓力衣游泳 Wear compression garments while swimming
- 避免氯含量過高的泳池 Avoid pools with excessive chlorine
- 游泳後立即清洗並塗抹保濕乳液 Rinse and moisturize immediately after swimming
- 如有皮膚破损或感染，暫停游泳 If skin breaks or infection, temporarily stop swimming

::: warning 運動禁忌

- 急性蜂窩織炎 Acute cellulitis
- 未控制的充血性心臟衰竭 Uncontrolled congestive heart failure
- 深部靜脈血栓（急性期）Deep vein thrombosis (acute phase)
- 未穩定的骨折 Unstable fractures
  :::

</details>

## 皮膚護理 Skin Care

<details>
<summary>基本護理原則 Basic Care Principles</summary>

皮膚護理是淋巴水腫管理的重要組成部分，可預防感染並維持皮膚完整性。Skin care is a critical component of lymphedema management, preventing infection and maintaining skin integrity.

| 護理項目 Care Item   | 建議 Recommendation                                | 頻率 Frequency                |
| -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| 清潔 Cleansing       | 溫和清潔劑，溫水清洗 Mild cleanser, lukewarm water | 每日 Daily                    |
| 保濕 Moisturizing    | 無香料乳液或乳霜 Fragrance-free lotion or cream    | 每日至少2次 At least 2x daily |
| 指甲護理 Nail care   | 修剪整齊，避免甲溝炎 Trim neatly, avoid paronychia | 每週 Weekly                   |
| 防曬 Sun protection  | SPF 30+ 防曬乳液 SPF 30+ sunscreen                 | 戶外時 When outdoors          |
| 角質處理 Callus care | 定期去除厚繭 Regular removal of thick calluses     | 每週 Weekly                   |

### 感染預防 Infection Prevention

淋巴水腫患者的感染風險顯著增加，特別是蜂窩織炎。Infection risk is significantly elevated in lymphedema patients, particularly cellulitis.

**預防措施 Preventive Measures：**

1. 保持皮膚清潔乾燥 Keep skin clean and dry
2. 每日檢查皮膚有無破损、紅腫、發熱 Inspect skin daily for breaks, redness, warmth
3. 及時處理任何皮膚破损 promptly treat any skin breaks
4. 使用抗生素軟膏於小伤口 Apply antibiotic ointment to minor wounds
5. 避免赤腳行走 Avoid walking barefoot
6. 避免在患側進行侵入性操作 Avoid invasive procedures on the affected side

::: warning 蜂窩織炎警示
如出現以下症狀，應立即就醫：Seek immediate medical attention if:

- 局部紅腫擴大 Expanding local redness
- 皮膚發熱 Skin warmth
- 疼痛加劇 Increasing pain
- 發燒 Fever
- 淋巴管炎（紅色條紋）Lymphangitis (red streaking)
  :::

### 指甲護理 Nail Care

- 保持指甲修剪整齊，避免過短 Keep nails trimmed neatly, avoid cutting too short
- 避免甲溝炎 Avoid paronychia
- 使用指甲銼刀而非指甲剪 Use nail file instead of nail clippers when possible
- 保持甲床清潔乾燥 Keep nail beds clean and dry

## 氣壓式循環治療 Pneumatic Compression Pump

<details>
<summary>治療機制 Mechanism of Action</summary>

氣壓式循環治療使用可充氣的袖套，以序貫性壓力促進淋巴液回流。Pneumatic compression therapy uses inflatable sleeves that apply sequential pressure to promote lymphatic return.

### 適應症 Indications

| 適應症 Indication                   | 說明 Description                                                    |
| ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 作為CDT的輔助治療 As adjunct to CDT | 增強消腫效果 Enhance decongestion                                   |
| 繃帶包紮困難 Bandaging difficulties | 患者無法自行包紮 Patients unable to self-bandage                    |
| 維持期治療 Maintenance therapy      | 無法穿著壓力衣物的患者 Patients unable to wear compression garments |
| 纖維化水腫 Fibrotic edema           | 輔助軟化硬組織 Help soften hard tissue                              |

### 治療參數 Treatment Parameters

| 參數 Parameter           | 建議值 Recommended Value                            |
| ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| 壓力 Pressure            | 30–60 mmHg                                          |
| 治療時間 Duration        | 30–60 分鐘 minutes                                  |
| 充氣節奏 Inflation cycle | 序貫式（遠端至近端）Sequential (distal to proximal) |
| 頻率 Frequency           | 每日或隔日 Daily or every other day                 |
| 療程 Course              | 4–12 週 weeks                                       |

### 證據 Evidence

- Cochrane系統性回顧顯示氣壓治療可減少肢體體積 Cochrane systematic review shows pneumatic compression reduces limb volume
- 可作為CDT的輔助治療 Can serve as adjunct to CDT
- 對於無法接受完整CDT的患者是替代選擇 Alternative for patients unable to receive full CDT

::: warning 氣壓治療禁忌

- 深部靜脈血栓（DVT）Deep vein thrombosis
- 充血性心臟衰竭 Congestive heart failure
- 急性蜂窩織炎 Acute cellulitis
- 未控制的高血壓 Uncontrolled hypertension
- 肺栓塞病史 History of pulmonary embolism
  :::

</details>

## 注意事項與禁忌症 Precautions and Contraindications

<details>
<summary>蜂窩織炎病史 History of Cellulitis</summary>

::: warning 蜂窩織炎管理
蜂窩織炎是淋巴水腫最常見的併發症，也是導致水腫惡化的主要因素。Cellulitis is the most common complication of lymphedema and a major factor in worsening edema.

**相對禁忌症 Relative Contraindication during acute infection：**

- 急性蜂窩織炎期間應暫停CDT治療（包括MLD和繃帶包紮）CDT (including MLD and bandaging) should be paused during acute cellulitis
- 治療應在抗生素治療24–48小時後、症狀改善時恢復 Treatment should resume 24–48 hours after starting antibiotics with symptom improvement
- 急性期應抬高患肢並使用抗生素 Elevate the affected limb and use antibiotics during the acute phase
  :::

</details>

### 深部靜脈血栓 DVT

<details>
<summary>深部靜脈血栓 DVT</summary>

深部靜脈血栓是淋巴水腫的重要鑑別診斷，也是絕對禁忌症。DVT is an important differential diagnosis for lymphedema and an absolute contraindication for treatment.

**臨床鑑別 Clinical Differentiation：**

| 特徵 Feature              | 淋巴水腫 Lymphedema                    | 深部靜脈血栓 DVT                |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| 發病 Onset                | 漸進性 Gradual                         | 急性 Acute                      |
| 疼痛 Pain                 | 輕微或無 Mild or none                  | 劇烈 Severe                     |
| 皮膚溫度 Skin temperature | 正常或略升 Normal or slightly elevated | 顯著升高 Significantly elevated |
| 皮膚顏色 Skin color       | 正常或輕微發紅 Normal or mild redness  | 紅紫 Red-purple                 |
| Homans徵 Homans sign      | 陰性 Negative                          | 可能陽性 Possibly positive      |
| 纖維蛋白原 D-dimer        | 正常 Normal                            | 升高 Elevated                   |

::: warning DVT 禁忌
如懷疑DVT，應立即進行超音波檢查並暫停所有淋巴水腫治療。DVT必須先治療，因為CDT可能導致血栓脫落，引發肺栓塞。If DVT is suspected, immediate ultrasound and cessation of all lymphedema treatment is required. DVT must be treated first as CDT may dislodge the thrombus, causing pulmonary embolism.
:::

</details>

### 充血性心臟衰竭 Congestive Heart Failure

<details>
<summary>充血性心臟衰竭 Congestive Heart Failure</summary>

- 淋巴水腫可與心臟衰竭共存 Lymphedema can coexist with heart failure
- CDT中的MLD和繃帶包紮可能增加心臟前負荷 MLD and bandaging in CDT may increase cardiac preload
- 需要心臟科與復健科共同管理 Requires joint management by cardiology and rehabilitation
- 壓力治療應在心臟功能穩定後開始 Pressure therapy should begin after cardiac function is stabilized

</details>

**ISL推薦的治療方案 ISL Recommended Treatment Protocol：**

1. **第一階段 Phase I（消腫期 Reduction）**

## 病患教育 Patient Education

<details>
<summary>自我管理要點 Self-Management Key Points</summary>

1. **每日皮膚檢查 Daily skin inspection** — 觀察有無破损、紅腫、發熱 Inspect for breaks, redness, warmth
2. **每日自我MLD Daily self-MLD** — 2–3次，每次15–20分鐘 2–3 times, 15–20 minutes each
3. **壓力衣物穿著 Compression garment wear** — 每日至少12小時 At least 12 hours daily
4. **規律運動 Regular exercise** — 每週至少3次，30分鐘 At least 3x/week, 30 minutes
5. **皮膚護理 Skin care** — 每日清潔和保濕 Daily cleansing and moisturizing
6. **體重管理 Weight management** — 維持健康體重 Maintain healthy weight
7. **避免損傷 Avoid trauma** — 避免患側測血壓、抽血 Avoid BP measurement, blood draws on affected side
8. **感染警示 Infection awareness** — 知道何時需要就醫 Know when to seek medical attention

::: warning 長期管理
淋巴水腫是一種終身疾病。患者需要理解，治療不是「治癒」而是「控制」。定期追蹤（至少每3–6個月一次）對於監測病情變化至關重要。Lymphedema is a lifelong condition. Patients need to understand that treatment is about "management" not "cure." Regular follow-up (at least every 3–6 months) is essential for monitoring changes.
:::

</details>
